File Naming Convention
======================

Exported emails:
  email_YYYYMMDD_HHMMSS_[subject].msg

Example:
  email_20260129_103045_Meeting_reminder.msg

Attachment folders:
  email_20260129_103045_Meeting_reminder_attachments/

Organized structure:
  BackupFolder/
    2026/
      01/
        email_20260129_103045_Meeting_reminder.msg
        email_20260129_103045_Meeting_reminder_attachments/
